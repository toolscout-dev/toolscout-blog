# About ToolScout

ToolScout is an independent resource for software comparisons, tool reviews, and free alternatives. We test and compare tools so you can make informed decisions without the noise of sponsored content.

Our reviews focus on practical use cases, honest pros and cons, and clear recommendations. No fluff, no affiliate bias — just useful information for people who want the right tool for the job.
